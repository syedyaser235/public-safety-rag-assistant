SOG 7.3 Vehicle Accidents

Vehicle Accidents

Arrival on Scene
A. The Driver Operator will position the apparatus in a way that will provide the best
protection for the crew during rescue and operation activities
B. The Officer will provide a scene size-up, assume command, determine type of incident,
number of vehicles involved, extent of damage to vehicles involved, extent of injuries,
and if extrication is needed
C. The Officer will request additional resources as needed (EMS, MedFlight)

Scene Safety
A. Ensure that unauthorized/untrained personnel do not enter the hazardous area
B. Provide High Visibility PPE and other safety measures for Traffic Hazards

Incident Actions
Command will assign teams as needed for:
- Fire and Hazard Control
- Extrication
- Patient Care
- Landing Zone
- Traffic Control
